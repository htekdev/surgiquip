## Project Overview

This regional medical center project included a full surgical program across 11 standard ORs, a dedicated hybrid OR, and a specialty cystoscopy room.

## Scope of Work

### Standard Operating Rooms (11 ORs)

- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — high-output lighting across all standard ORs
- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns
- **Skytron 6701 Surgical Tables** — advanced multi-specialty positioning
- **SkyVision Integration** — unified OR video management

### Hybrid Operating Room

- Phillips imaging integration with ceiling-mount infrastructure
- Aurora LED lights with imaging-cone clearance

### Cystoscopy Room

- **Skytron 3600 Cystoscopy Table** — purpose-built table for urological procedures
- Dedicated lighting and boom configuration for cystoscopy workflow

## Project Highlights

- **Multi-specialty coverage** — standard, hybrid, and cystoscopy rooms under one program
- **Mixed table deployment** — 6701 general tables paired with 3600 specialty cystoscopy table
