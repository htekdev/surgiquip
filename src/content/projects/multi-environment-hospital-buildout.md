## Project Overview

Multi-environment hospital project spanning surgical, obstetrics, trauma, and emergency departments.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (5 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — high-output surgical lighting
- **Surgical Tables** — multi-specialty positioning

### Trauma Rooms (2 Rooms)

- Trauma-grade ceiling exam lights and equipment carrier systems

### LDRP Suites (14 Rooms)

- **Skytron AR24 Birthing Lights** — wand-controlled ceiling-mount birthing lights

### Emergency Department (18 Bays)

- Ceiling exam lights and equipment carriers across all bays

## Project Highlights

- **Four environments, one standard** — OR, trauma, LDRP, and ED under a single equipment program
