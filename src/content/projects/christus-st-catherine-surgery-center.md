## Project Overview

CHRISTUS St. Catherine Surgery Center provides ambulatory surgical services for the West Houston and Katy area communities. Surgiquip Solutions delivered a complete surgical suite outfitting — serving as the single vendor for OR equipment, lights, sterile processing infrastructure, and surgical support systems.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (4 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount gas, electrical, and data service columns providing anesthesia and surgical service access at all table positions
- **Skytron 6700B Surgical Tables** — high-flexibility surgical tables supporting a full range of general, orthopedic, and minimally invasive procedures
- **Skytron Stellar Surgical Lights** — LED surgical illumination with satellite head configuration for shadow-free visualization

### Sterile Processing & Support

- **Sterilizers** — autoclave sterilization equipment for the sterile processing department
- **Scrub sinks** — stainless steel surgical scrub stations at OR corridors
- **Instrument washer** — automated instrument decontamination for efficient sterile processing workflow

## Project Highlights

- **Single-vendor scope** — Surgiquip supplied OR equipment, lights, and sterile processing infrastructure under one contract, simplifying construction coordination
- **6700B tables** — high-performance surgical tables supporting the full procedure mix typical of a community surgery center

## Outcome

CHRISTUS St. Catherine Surgery Center opened with all surgical systems commissioned and staff trained. The complete Skytron infrastructure — booms, tables, and lights — provides a consistent, serviceable platform maintained under Surgiquip's local service program.
