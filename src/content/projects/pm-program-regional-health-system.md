## Program Overview

A regional Texas health system operating two hospital campuses needed a reliable PM program for their surgical equipment fleet — eight ORs, a mix of Skytron table generations, Aurora LED lights, and Center Mount booms, all split across two sites.

Surgiquip manages this contract: bi-annual on-site service visits, emergency response coverage, and proactive parts management across both campuses.

## Program Structure

Each bi-annual visit covers all eight ORs at both campuses:

- **Tables** — all axes of movement, weight capacity validation, accessory attachment verification, lubrication
- **Lights** — LED output measurement, arm tension, sterilization handle inspection
- **Booms** — gas outlet flow testing, electrical outlet testing, structural integrity check
- **Documentation** — written PM report per piece of equipment, calibration tags, formatted for Joint Commission / DNV / CMS survey readiness

Emergency response is included: 24-hour technician coverage, Skytron parts stock, loaner coordination if needed.

## Active Status

Both campuses are maintained in survey-ready condition. Emergency response coverage has resolved critical equipment issues with minimal downtime since program inception.
