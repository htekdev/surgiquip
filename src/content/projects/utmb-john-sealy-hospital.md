## Project Overview

UTMB John Sealy Hospital is the flagship teaching hospital of the University of Texas Medical Branch in Galveston — one of the oldest medical schools in Texas and a Level I Trauma Center. The scale and complexity of the John Sealy surgical program required a comprehensive Skytron equipment deployment spanning multiple OR generations, a catheterization laboratory, and a full LDRP floor.

Surgiquip Solutions served as the Skytron equipment partner for the John Sealy program, delivering equipment across multiple generations of the Skytron table product line and a specialized 360° ceiling boom solution for the complex OR environments.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (32 ORs)

- **Center Mount Equipment Booms — 360° Configuration** — specialized boom mounting configuration providing full 360° rotation capability, allowing surgical and anesthesia teams to position service columns at any point around the table without boom conflict
- **Skytron Stellar Surgical Lights** — LED surgical illumination across the OR suite
- **Multi-generation Skytron Table Deployment** — John Sealy's complex, multi-specialty surgical program required tables spanning the full Skytron table range:
  - **3000 and 3600 Series** — specialty table configurations for urologic and orthopedic procedures
  - **6000 and 6500 Series** — versatile surgical tables for general and mixed-case surgical programs
  - **6600 and 6700 Series** — high-flexibility advanced surgical tables for complex and specialty procedures

### Catheterization Laboratory (1 Lab)

- Ceiling-mount boom infrastructure and equipment carriers for interventional cardiology

### LDRP Suites (23 Rooms)

- **Skytron AR24 Ceiling Birthing Lights** — ceiling-mounted birthing lights for UTMB's obstetrics program, avoiding the door-track mechanisms that cause dust accumulation and jamming in traditional birthing light systems

## Project Highlights

- **360° boom solution** — the full-rotation boom configuration is a specialized engineering solution for teaching hospital environments where equipment positioning flexibility is critical for resident training and complex procedure set-ups
- **Multi-generation table range** — Surgiquip's expertise across the full Skytron table product line allowed appropriate table selection for each specialty OR
- **UTMB scale** — 32 ORs, cath lab, and 23 LDRP suites represents one of Surgiquip's largest single-campus deployments

## Outcome

UTMB John Sealy's surgical, interventional, and obstetric programs were fully outfitted and commissioned. The John Sealy project extended Surgiquip's service footprint to Galveston County and established a long-term relationship with the University of Texas Medical Branch.
