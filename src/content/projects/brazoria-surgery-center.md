## Project Overview

Brazoria Surgery Center serves the Brazoria County community as an ambulatory surgical facility, providing outpatient surgical services close to home for patients in Brazoria and surrounding communities. Surgiquip Solutions supplied and installed the core surgical infrastructure — designed for both immediate clinical use and future scalability.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (4 ORs)

- **Boom-ready ceiling infrastructure** — structural support and service rough-in for future ceiling-mount equipment boom installation as case volume grows
- **Skytron Stellar Surgical Lights** — high-output LED lights for ambulatory surgical illumination across all 4 ORs

### Procedure Rooms (2 Rooms)

- Procedure-grade lighting for endoscopy and minor procedure workflows

### Support Spaces

- **Scrub sinks** — stainless surgical scrub sinks at OR approach corridors
- **Warming cabinets** — blanket and fluid warming units for patient comfort and temperature management
- **Instrument washer** — automated instrument decontamination for sterile processing workflow

## Project Highlights

- **Future-growth design** — boom-mount infrastructure installed at build-out allows ceiling boom addition without structural disruption as case volume increases
- **Complete supply scope** — Surgiquip served as a single vendor for lights, sinks, warmers, and sterile processing equipment

## Outcome

Brazoria Surgery Center opened with a complete, operationally ready surgical suite. The boom-ready infrastructure protects the facility's investment by enabling ceiling-mount addition without ceiling reconstruction.
