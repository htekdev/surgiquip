## Project Overview

This academic medical center campus project required outfitting a complete surgical, interventional, and obstetrics program. The project spanned multiple Skytron table generations and required a 360-degree rotating boom solution — one of the most versatile ceiling-mount configurations available.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (32 ORs)

- **Multi-generation surgical tables** — Skytron 3500, 6500, 6700B, and 6701 tables deployed across different surgical specialties
- **360-Degree Rotating Center Mount Booms** — full-rotation ceiling boom system providing maximum positioning flexibility
- **Skytron Stellar Surgical Lights** — high-output LED lighting across all ORs

### Catheterization Laboratory (1 Lab)

- Ceiling-mount equipment carrier and service infrastructure for interventional cardiology

### LDRP Suites (23 Rooms)

- **Skytron AR24 Birthing Lights** — wand-controlled ceiling-mount birthing lights
- Warm, patient-centered lighting design for labor, delivery, recovery, and postpartum care

## Project Highlights

- **Multi-generation table deployment** — four Skytron table generations matched to specialty requirements
- **360-degree boom innovation** — rotating center mount booms provide maximum flexibility
- **Academic mission support** — equipment selection accommodated teaching and training workflows
