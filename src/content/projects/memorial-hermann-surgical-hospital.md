## Project Overview

Memorial Hermann Surgical Hospital is a dedicated surgical facility within the Memorial Hermann Health System, offering focused surgical care in a specialized environment. As part of the system's broader relationship with Surgiquip Solutions, the Surgical Hospital was outfitted with the Memorial Hermann standard Skytron equipment platform.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (6 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns delivering medical gas, electrical, and data services for anesthesia and surgical teams
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — high-output, high-color-rendering LED surgical illumination consistent with the Aurora LED platform used throughout the Memorial Hermann system

## Project Highlights

- **Dedicated surgical facility** — purpose-built surgical hospital environment with consistent, high-performance OR infrastructure
- **System-wide consistency** — Aurora LED and Center Mount Boom platform matches the equipment standard across Surgiquip's Memorial Hermann portfolio, simplifying cross-facility staff orientation and PM scheduling

## Outcome

Memorial Hermann Surgical Hospital's 6-OR program was commissioned and cleared for clinical operations. The facility operates as part of the Memorial Hermann network's surgical capacity, maintained under Surgiquip's ongoing service relationship with the health system.
