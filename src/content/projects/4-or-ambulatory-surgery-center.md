## Project Overview

A new ambulatory surgery center designed for both immediate clinical use and future scalability.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (4 ORs)

- **Boom-ready ceiling infrastructure** — structural support for future ceiling-mount boom installation
- **Skytron Stellar Surgical Lights** — high-output LED lights across all 4 ORs

### Procedure Rooms (2 Rooms)

- Procedure-grade lighting for endoscopy and minor procedures

### Support Spaces

- **Scrub sinks** — stainless surgical scrub sinks
- **Warming cabinets** — blanket and fluid warming
- **Instrument washer** — automated decontamination

## Project Highlights

- **Future-growth design** — boom-mount infrastructure allows ceiling boom addition without structural disruption
- **Complete supply scope** — single vendor for lights, sinks, warmers, and sterile processing
