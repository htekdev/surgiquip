## Project Overview

Memorial Hermann Sugar Land Hospital serves the Fort Bend County community — one of the fastest-growing counties in Texas — with a full inpatient and surgical program. Surgiquip Solutions outfitted the hospital's surgical suite and endoscopy suite as part of the Memorial Hermann system's equipment standard, consistent with the Skytron platform maintained across Surgiquip's Memorial Hermann portfolio.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (8 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns providing gas, electrical, and data services for anesthesia and surgical positions
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — consistent with the Aurora LED standard deployed across the Memorial Hermann system
- **Skytron 6701 Surgical Tables** — versatile surgical tables supporting the general, orthopedic, and minimally invasive procedure mix
- **SkyVision Integration** — OR video management and documentation system for surgical recording and team communication

### Endoscopy Suite (1 Suite)

- Equipment carrier and lighting infrastructure for gastrointestinal endoscopy workflow

## Project Highlights

- **SkyVision integration** — unified OR video documentation standard across all 8 ORs
- **Memorial Hermann portfolio continuity** — consistent with the Skytron equipment standard deployed across Surgiquip's 13-location Memorial Hermann engagement in the Houston area

## Outcome

Memorial Hermann Sugar Land's surgical program was commissioned and cleared for clinical use, extending the Memorial Hermann system equipment standard into the Fort Bend County market. Surgiquip maintains the Skytron equipment under the ongoing system-wide service relationship.
