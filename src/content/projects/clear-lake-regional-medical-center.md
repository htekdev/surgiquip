## Project Overview

Clear Lake Regional Medical Center, serving the Webster and Clear Lake communities south of Houston, engaged Surgiquip Solutions to outfit its complete surgical and obstetrics program. The project spanned three distinct clinical environments — standard operating rooms, C-section suites, and LDRP (Labor, Delivery, Recovery, Postpartum) suites — each with distinct equipment and lighting requirements.

## Scope of Work

### Standard Operating Rooms (10 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns delivering gas, electrical, and data services to anesthesia and surgical positions
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — high-output LED lighting with shadow-fill secondary heads across all 10 ORs
- **Skytron 6701 Surgical Tables** — versatile surgical tables supporting orthopedic, general, and minimally invasive procedures
- **SkyVision Integration** — OR video management for surgical documentation and team communication

### C-Section Suites (3 Rooms)

- Center Mount Boom configuration adapted for obstetric surgical environments
- Aurora LED lights — dual-head configuration for open-field cesarean visualization
- 6701 tables with obstetric positioning features

### LDRP Suites (6 Rooms)

- Ceiling-mount service infrastructure for laboring and postpartum patient environments
- Lighting and equipment carrier configuration for combined labor, delivery, and recovery use

## Project Highlights

- **Three-environment project** — simultaneous outfitting of OR, C-section, and LDRP environments required careful configuration differentiation
- **SkyVision integration** — unified OR video standard across all 10 standard ORs
- **Aurora LED** — consistent lighting platform simplifies ongoing PM and bulb standardization

## Outcome

Clear Lake Regional's surgical and obstetrics program was commissioned and opened to clinical use with all Skytron systems installed, calibrated, and staff-trained. The Aurora LED platform across ORs and C-section rooms delivers the consistent illumination standard the surgical team requires.
