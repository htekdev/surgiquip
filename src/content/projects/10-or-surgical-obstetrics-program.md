## Project Overview

This regional medical center project spanned the complete surgical and obstetrics program — from standard operating rooms through C-section suites and LDRP birthing rooms.

## Scope of Work

### Standard Operating Rooms (10 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount gas, electrical, and data columns
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — high-output LED lighting across all ORs
- **Skytron 6701 Surgical Tables** — advanced multi-specialty positioning
- **SkyVision Integration** — OR video management and documentation

### C-Section Suites (3 Rooms)

- Surgical-grade ceiling lighting and boom infrastructure for obstetric surgical workflow
- Equipment optimized for C-section rapid-response scenarios

### LDRP Suites (6 Rooms)

- **Skytron AR24 Birthing Lights** — wand-controlled ceiling-mount birthing lights
- Warm, patient-centered lighting for labor, delivery, recovery, and postpartum

## Project Highlights

- **Surgical + obstetrics under one program** — unified equipment standard from OR through LDRP
- **SkyVision throughout** — video and documentation integration across all surgical rooms
- **AR24 birthing lights** — ceiling-mount design eliminates dust-accumulation issues
