## Project Overview

Memorial Hermann Orthopedic & Spine Hospital is a dedicated specialty surgical facility focused on high-volume orthopedic and spine surgery programs. The specialized procedure mix — total joints, spine reconstruction, sports medicine — places specific demands on OR infrastructure: precise lighting with documentation capability, flexible surgical tables for complex patient positioning, and efficient nursing workflows.

Surgiquip Solutions configured the 10-OR surgical suite to meet these orthopedic specialty requirements.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (10 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns positioned for the anesthesia and surgical team configurations typical of orthopedic procedures
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights with In-Light Camera** — surgical lighting with integrated camera for real-time surgical video capture and documentation; particularly valuable for orthopedic procedure documentation and training
- **Skytron 6700B Surgical Tables** — high-flexibility tables with broad positioning range supporting lateral decubitus, prone, and beach-chair positions common in orthopedic surgery
- **Nurse Documentation Centers** — wall-mounted or mobile nursing documentation stations streamlining charting and order entry during procedure

## Project Highlights

- **In-light camera integration** — Aurora LED lights with built-in camera allow real-time surgical video without a separate ceiling camera system
- **Orthopedic-spec 6700B tables** — 6700B positioning flexibility supports the complex patient positioning requirements of spine and joint replacement procedures
- **Nurse documentation centers** — purpose-built documentation positions reduce nursing workflow interruption during procedure

## Outcome

Memorial Hermann Orthopedic & Spine Hospital's 10-OR suite was commissioned and cleared for the facility's high-volume orthopedic and spine surgery program. The Aurora LED with in-light camera has become a valuable resource for the facility's surgical education and quality assurance programs.
