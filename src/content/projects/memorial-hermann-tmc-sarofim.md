## Project Overview

The Sarofim Pavilion at Memorial Hermann Texas Medical Center is one of the most ambitious surgical and emergency care build-outs in Houston's history — and one of Surgiquip Solutions' defining projects. When Memorial Hermann needed a single, accountable partner to outfit an entire new hospital tower across dozens of operating environments, they turned to Surgiquip.

As the authorized Skytron dealer for Southeast Texas, Surgiquip served as the equipment integration lead for every surgical and emergency room in the Sarofim Pavilion — coordinating procurement, delivery sequencing, installation, commissioning, and staff training across a complex, phased construction schedule.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Room Suite (36 ORs)

- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — installed across all 36 standard operating rooms, delivering high-color-rendering illumination critical for complex open procedures
- **Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount configuration providing gas, electrical, and data services for anesthesia and surgical teams
- **Anesthesia Booms** — dedicated ceiling-mount anesthesia service columns keeping floor space clear
- **Large Screen Monitor Booms** — ceiling-suspended imaging monitor arms for surgical video and documentation

### Hybrid Operating Suites (3 Rooms)

- Full ceiling-mount boom infrastructure supporting fixed C-arm imaging integration
- Aurora LED lights engineered to clear the imaging cone for interventional cardiology and vascular procedures
- Extended reach monitor boom arms for dual-screen imaging displays

### Catheterization Laboratories (2 Labs)

- Equipment carriers and ceiling-mount service infrastructure for interventional cardiology
- Coordinated with cardiac imaging vendor equipment layout

### Emergency Department

- **Adult ED (20 bays)** — ceiling exam lights and equipment carriers
- **Critical ED (16 bays)** — high-acuity ceiling lighting and service infrastructure
- **Pediatric ED (11 bays)** — pediatric-spec lighting configurations
- **Trauma Rooms (9 rooms)** — trauma-grade ceiling exam lights and carrier systems
- **Forensic Suite (1 room)** — specialty lighting installation

## Project Highlights

- **Scale** — One of the largest single-contract OR outfitting projects in Houston history
- **Aurora LED throughout** — consistent lighting standard across all 36 ORs, reducing staff training complexity and PM program scope
- **Phased coordination** — Surgiquip managed equipment deliveries across multiple construction phases to align with facility activation schedules
- **Single vendor accountability** — one contract covering OR, hybrid, cath lab, and emergency department equipment

## Outcome

The Sarofim Pavilion opened as a flagship clinical facility for Memorial Hermann Health System, with every surgical, interventional, and emergency environment commissioned and cleared for clinical use. The Aurora LED platform across all 36 ORs established a consistent, maintainable standard for the system's largest campus.
