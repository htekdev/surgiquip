## Project Overview

St. Luke's at the Vintage serves the Northwest Houston community as a community hospital offering surgical, obstetric, and emergency services. Surgiquip Solutions outfitted the facility's complete clinical program — from standard operating rooms and trauma suites through the LDRP floor and the full emergency department.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (5 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns delivering gas, electrical, and data services for surgical and anesthesia teams
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — high-output LED illumination for the full general surgery procedure mix

### Trauma Rooms (2 Rooms)

- Center Mount Booms configured for trauma patient access
- Aurora LED ceiling exam lights for high-acuity trauma assessment and intervention

### LDRP Suites (14 Rooms)

- **Skytron AR24 Ceiling Birthing Lights** — ceiling-mounted birthing lights providing positioning flexibility without door-track mechanisms
- Combined labor, delivery, recovery, and postpartum use in each suite

### Emergency Department (18 Bays)

- **Ceiling Exam Lights** — articulating ceiling-mounted exam lights for efficient emergency room assessment workflows across all 18 bays

## Project Highlights

- **Four clinical environments** — simultaneous outfitting of surgical, trauma, obstetric, and emergency environments under one project
- **AR24 birthing lights** — ceiling-mount design consistent with Surgiquip's obstetric lighting approach across multiple Houston-area facilities
- **18-bay ED** — large emergency department capacity serving Northwest Houston's growing population

## Outcome

St. Luke's at the Vintage opened with all surgical, obstetric, and emergency environments commissioned and cleared for clinical use. The facility serves as a full-service community hospital for the Vintage, Willowbrook, and Northwest Houston communities.
