## Project Overview

UTMB Specialty Care Center at Victory Lakes is an ambulatory specialty care facility serving the League City, Clear Lake, and Galveston County communities — extending UTMB Health's specialty surgical services closer to the southern Houston suburbs. Surgiquip Solutions outfitted the facility's 4-OR surgical suite with the Skytron equipment standard consistent with the broader UTMB Health relationship established at John Sealy Hospital.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (4 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns providing gas, electrical, and data access for anesthesia and surgical teams
- **Skytron Stellar Surgical Lights** — LED surgical illumination for the specialty procedure mix
- **Nurse Documentation Centers** — purpose-built nursing documentation positions for efficient charting during procedure
- **Stainless Steel Storage Cabinets** — clinical-grade stainless OR supply cabinets

## Project Highlights

- **UTMB system continuity** — equipment consistent with the John Sealy Skytron standard, simplifying UTMB Health's system-wide service and parts programs
- **League City market** — extends Surgiquip's Galveston County service reach into the Victory Lakes and Clear Lake communities

## Outcome

UTMB Specialty Care Center at Victory Lakes was commissioned with all OR systems installed and staff trained. The facility provides specialty surgical care to Galveston County patients who previously required travel to UTMB's Galveston campus.
