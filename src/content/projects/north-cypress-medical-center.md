## Project Overview

North Cypress Medical Center serves the Cypress and Northwest Houston community as a full-service community hospital. Surgiquip Solutions outfitted the hospital's 6-OR surgical suite with a complete Skytron equipment program — including the SkyVision OR video integration system and stainless steel clinical storage cabinets.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (6 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns providing gas, electrical, and data services for anesthesia and surgical positions
- **Skytron Stellar Surgical Lights** — LED surgical lighting with multi-axis shadow control across all 6 ORs
- **Dual Monitor Boom Arms** — ceiling-suspended dual-screen video arms for laparoscopic and robotic imaging display
- **SkyVision Integration System** — OR video management for surgical documentation, live streaming to family waiting areas, and team communication
- **Stainless Steel Storage Cabinets** — clinical-grade stainless supply cabinets for OR supply management

## Project Highlights

- **Dual monitor configuration** — ceiling-suspended dual screens support the minimally invasive procedure mix increasingly common at community hospitals
- **SkyVision integration** — comprehensive OR video management including surgical recording and family viewing capability
- **Stainless steel cabinets** — clinical-grade storage matching the durability and infection-control requirements of the OR environment

## Outcome

North Cypress Medical Center's surgical suite was commissioned and cleared for clinical use. The SkyVision integration and dual monitor configuration support a full minimally invasive surgery program serving Northwest Houston patients.
