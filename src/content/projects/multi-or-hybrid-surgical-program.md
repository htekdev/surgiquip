## Project Overview

This project brought hybrid OR capability to a growing suburban hospital campus, combining standard surgical suites with a dedicated hybrid OR for interventional procedures.

## Scope of Work

### Standard Operating Rooms

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — high-output LED lighting
- **Skytron 6701 Surgical Tables** — advanced surgical positioning
- **SkyVision Integration** — OR video management system

### Hybrid Operating Room

- Phillips imaging integration with ceiling-mount boom infrastructure
- Aurora LED lights with imaging-cone clearance positioning
- Extended monitor arms for interventional imaging displays

## Project Highlights

- **Suburban hybrid capability** — advanced interventional OR capability in a community setting
- **Consistent equipment standard** — same platform across all rooms
- **Imaging coordination** — managed Phillips imaging integration throughout construction
