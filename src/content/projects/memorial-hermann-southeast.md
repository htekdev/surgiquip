## Project Overview

Memorial Hermann Southeast serves the southeast Houston community, offering a full inpatient and surgical program including specialty services in vascular surgery and urology. Surgiquip Solutions outfitted the hospital's complete surgical suite — standard ORs, a hybrid vascular OR with Phillips imaging integration, and a dedicated cystoscopy room.

## Scope of Work

### Standard Operating Rooms (11 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns providing anesthesia and surgical team gas, electrical, and data access
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — high-output LED illumination across all 11 standard ORs
- **Skytron 6701 Surgical Tables** — versatile surgical tables supporting general surgery, gynecologic, and minimally invasive procedures

### Hybrid Operating Room (1 Room — Skytron/Phillips Integration)

- **Ceiling-mount boom infrastructure** — engineered to clear the Phillips interventional imaging arc while maintaining full boom service reach
- **Aurora LED lights** — positioned for C-arm rotation clearance
- Coordination with Phillips installation team for room layout and ceiling coordinate planning

### Cystoscopy Room (1 Room)

- **Skytron 3600 Specialty Table** — urologic procedure table configuration optimized for cystoscopy and urologic procedures
- Procedure lighting and equipment carrier for urology workflow

## Project Highlights

- **Phillips hybrid integration** — vascular and interventional procedures supported by Phillips imaging in a fully integrated hybrid OR environment
- **Multi-specialty configuration** — surgical, vascular, and urologic ORs outfitted under one project scope
- **6701 + 3600 table mix** — appropriate table selection per OR specialty type

## Outcome

Memorial Hermann Southeast's complete surgical program — standard ORs, hybrid suite, and cystoscopy room — was commissioned and cleared for clinical use. The hybrid OR expanded the facility's vascular surgery capability, reducing patient transfers to the Texas Medical Center for interventional procedures.
