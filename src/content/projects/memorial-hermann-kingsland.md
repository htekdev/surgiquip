## Project Overview

Memorial Hermann Kingsland Surgery Center is part of the Memorial Hermann Health System's network of outpatient surgical facilities serving the West Houston and Katy corridor. Surgiquip Solutions handled the complete OR equipment program — delivering and installing the core surgical infrastructure for all four operating rooms and two procedure rooms.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (4 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns providing medical gas, electrical, and data access for anesthesia and surgical teams
- **Skytron Stellar Surgical Lights** — high-output LED illumination with multi-axis shadow control
- **Skytron 6700B Surgical Tables** — high-flexibility tables supporting the full elective procedure mix typical of a Memorial Hermann ambulatory center

### Procedure Rooms (2 Rooms)

- Procedure-grade lighting for endoscopy and minor procedure workflows

### Sterile Processing

- **Sterilizers** — autoclave sterilization for the sterile processing department
- **Instrument washer** — automated instrument decontamination supporting surgical instrument throughput

## Project Highlights

- **Memorial Hermann network standard** — as part of the Memorial Hermann system, Kingsland follows the same Skytron equipment platform maintained across Surgiquip's Memorial Hermann portfolio, simplifying service and PM programs
- **Full-scope single vendor** — OR equipment, lights, sterile processing, and procedure room infrastructure under one contract

## Outcome

Memorial Hermann Kingsland Surgery Center opened with a complete, commissioned surgical suite consistent with the Memorial Hermann system equipment standard. Surgiquip maintains the Skytron equipment under the system's ongoing service relationship.
