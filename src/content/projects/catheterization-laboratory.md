## Project Overview

Precise engineering of ceiling-mount equipment around a Siemens bi-plane C-arm imaging system — one of the most space-constrained clinical environments.

## Scope of Work

### Catheterization Laboratory

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms (Extended Reach)** — engineered to clear the bi-plane imaging envelope
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — positioned to avoid interference with imaging arcs
- **Siemens Bi-Plane C-Arm Integration** — ceiling infrastructure designed around specific dimensional requirements
- **Anesthesia Boom** — dedicated ceiling-mount service column

## Project Highlights

- **Imaging integration precision** — boom positions calculated to clear the C-arm's full imaging arc
- **Extended reach arms** — custom arm lengths for cath lab spatial constraints
- **Vendor coordination** — installation coordinated with Siemens imaging team
