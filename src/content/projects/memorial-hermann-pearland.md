## Project Overview

Memorial Hermann Pearland serves the Pearland and Brazoria County community — one of the fastest-growing suburban corridors in the Houston area. When Memorial Hermann expanded its Pearland emergency services, Surgiquip Solutions supplied and installed the clinical equipment infrastructure consistent with the Memorial Hermann system standard.

## Scope of Work

### Emergency Rooms (3 Rooms)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns providing medical gas, electrical, and IV access for emergency clinical workflows
- **Aurora LED Exam Lights** — high-output, articulating exam lighting for emergency assessment and procedure illumination
- **Warming Cabinets** — blanket and IV fluid warming for patient temperature management in emergency care
- **Storage Cabinets** — clinical supply storage for emergency room consumables and medications
- **Portable Exam Lights** — supplemental mobile lighting for bedside procedures and examination

## Project Highlights

- **Memorial Hermann system standard** — consistent with the Skytron equipment platform used across the Memorial Hermann network, simplifying maintenance and parts programs
- **Pearland market** — extends Surgiquip's Southeast Texas service reach into Brazoria County's growing healthcare corridor

## Outcome

Memorial Hermann Pearland's emergency rooms were commissioned and equipped to deliver emergency care consistent with the Memorial Hermann system standard. Surgiquip maintains the equipment under the ongoing service relationship with Memorial Hermann Health System.
