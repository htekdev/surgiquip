## Project Overview

This project outfitted a dedicated heart and vascular institute with advanced cardiovascular operating rooms and a hybrid OR featuring integrated imaging capabilities.

## Scope of Work

### Cardiovascular Operating Rooms (4 CV ORs)

- **Heavy-duty Center Mount Booms** — engineered for cardiovascular surgery equipment arrays
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — high-color-rendering illumination critical for cardiovascular visualization
- **Perfusion equipment boom arms** — dedicated ceiling-mount arms for heart-lung bypass equipment
- **Extended reach anesthesia booms** — accommodating complex cardiac anesthesia requirements

### Hybrid Operating Room (1 Hybrid OR)

- Full ceiling-mount boom system engineered around imaging equipment footprint
- Aurora LED lights with imaging-cone clearance positioning
- Extended reach monitor boom arms for dual-screen angiographic imaging

## Project Highlights

- **Cardiac specialization** — equipment optimized for open-heart, vascular, and catheter-based procedures
- **Hybrid flexibility** — seamless transition between open surgical and catheter-based interventional workflows
- **Imaging integration** — precise coordination with imaging vendor for ceiling-mount clearance
