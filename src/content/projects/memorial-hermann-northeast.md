## Project Overview

Memorial Hermann Northeast serves the Humble and North Houston corridor as a full-service community hospital. Surgiquip Solutions outfitted the hospital's surgical program — delivering standard OR infrastructure and a hybrid OR with Phillips imaging system integration for interventional vascular and cardiac procedures.

## Scope of Work

### Standard Operating Rooms

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns providing gas, electrical, and data access for anesthesia and surgical positions
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — high-output LED surgical illumination across all standard ORs
- **Skytron 6701 Surgical Tables** — versatile surgical tables supporting the general surgery, orthopedic, and minimally invasive procedure mix

### Hybrid Operating Room (1 Room — Skytron/Phillips Integration)

- **Ceiling-mount boom system** — engineered to clear the Phillips imaging arc while providing full boom service reach at all table positions
- **Aurora LED lights** — positioned for imaging-cone clearance, maintaining full illumination at all C-arm positions
- Coordination with Phillips installation team for integrated room layout

## Project Highlights

- **Phillips integration** — Surgiquip managed the Skytron ceiling-mount scope in coordination with the Phillips interventional imaging system, ensuring compatible ceiling infrastructure
- **North Houston market** — Northeast extends Surgiquip's Memorial Hermann relationship into the Humble and North Houston service area

## Outcome

Memorial Hermann Northeast's surgical program, including the hybrid OR, was commissioned and cleared for clinical use. The hybrid suite supports interventional vascular and cardiac procedures for North Houston patients who previously required transfer to the Texas Medical Center.
