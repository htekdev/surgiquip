## Project Overview

One of our most comprehensive single-facility outfitting projects — spanning every surgical, interventional, and emergency environment in a major community hospital campus.

## Scope of Work

### Standard Operating Rooms (18 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount gas, electrical, and data service columns
- **Skytron 6700B Surgical Tables** — high-flexibility tables supporting a wide range of procedures
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — consistent high-output lighting across all standard ORs
- **SkyVision Integration** — OR video management and documentation system

### Cardiovascular Operating Rooms (4 CV ORs)

- Heavy-duty ceiling-mount boom infrastructure supporting open-heart and vascular surgery equipment
- Aurora LED lights configured for cardiovascular visualization requirements
- Extended reach equipment booms accommodating perfusion equipment positioning

### Hybrid Operating Rooms (2 Hybrid ORs)

- Full ceiling-mount boom system engineered around Siemens Zeego robotic C-arm footprint
- Aurora LED lights with imaging-cone clearance positioning
- Extended monitor boom arms for dual-screen angiographic imaging

### Catheterization Laboratory (1 Lab)

- Ceiling-mount equipment carrier and service infrastructure

### Emergency Department (4 Trauma + 12 ER Bays)

- Trauma room ceiling exam lights and equipment carriers
- Emergency bay ceiling lighting for 12 standard treatment bays

## Project Highlights

- **Hybrid integration expertise** — Siemens Zeego robotic imaging systems require precise ceiling-mount coordination
- **Multi-service-line coordination** — simultaneous outfitting of surgical, cardiovascular, hybrid, and emergency environments
- **SkyVision throughout** — unified video and documentation standard across 18 rooms
