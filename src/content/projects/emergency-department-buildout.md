## Project Overview

Extended a standardized equipment platform to a suburban hospital's emergency department.

## Scope of Work

### Emergency Rooms

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount equipment carriers
- **Aurora LED Exam Lights** — high-output ceiling-mount examination lights
- **Warming Cabinets** — blanket and fluid warming
- **Storage Cabinets** — medical supply storage
- **Portable Exam Lights** — mobile examination lights

## Project Highlights

- **Standardized platform** — same Aurora LED and Center Mount Boom standard used across the broader health system
- **Complete bay outfitting** — lighting, warmers, and storage in every bay
