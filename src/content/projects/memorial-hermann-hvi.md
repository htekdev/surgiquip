## Project Overview

Memorial Hermann Heart & Vascular Institute is one of the most respected cardiovascular programs in Texas — and the surgical infrastructure Surgiquip Solutions installed supports that clinical reputation. When Memorial Hermann built out the HVI surgical floor, Surgiquip served as the Skytron equipment integration contractor for the cardiovascular ORs and the hybrid suite.

## Scope of Work

### Cardiovascular Operating Rooms (4 CV ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — heavy-duty ceiling-mount service columns supporting the complex equipment demands of open-heart and vascular procedures
- **Skytron 6700B Surgical Tables** — high-flexibility surgical tables with cardiovascular procedure positioning capabilities
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — high-output, high-color-rendering lights for cardiovascular visualization
- **SkyVision Integration** — OR video management for surgical documentation and team communication

### Hybrid Operating Room (1 Room — Skytron/Siemens Integration)

- **Full ceiling-mount boom system** engineered around the Siemens imaging footprint — maintaining imaging cone clearance while providing full anesthesia and surgical service access
- **Aurora LED lights** — positioned to clear C-arm rotation arc during interventional procedures
- **Dual monitor boom arms** — ceiling-suspended dual-screen configuration for angiographic imaging and hemodynamic data

## Project Highlights

- **Hybrid OR precision** — Siemens integration required exact ceiling-mount coordinate planning to maintain the full imaging cone while providing boom service reach at all table positions
- **Cardiovascular-grade equipment** — 6700B table series and heavy-duty boom configuration support the extended positioning demands of cardiac surgery
- **Consistent lighting standard** — Aurora LED across all 5 rooms simplifies staff orientation and PM contracts

## Outcome

The HVI surgical floor was commissioned and cleared for open-heart, vascular, and interventional cardiology procedures. The hybrid OR supports a combined cardiac surgery and interventional cardiology workflow — one of the more complex integration environments in the Houston market.
