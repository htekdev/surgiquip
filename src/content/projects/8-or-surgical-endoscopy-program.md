## Project Overview

This community hospital project included a full 8-OR surgical program plus a dedicated endoscopy suite.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (8 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — high-output LED lighting
- **Skytron 6701 Surgical Tables** — multi-specialty positioning
- **SkyVision Integration** — OR video management and documentation

### Endoscopy Suite

- Purpose-configured lighting and equipment for endoscopic procedures

## Project Highlights

- **Surgical + endoscopy standardization** — same equipment platform across all procedural rooms
- **SkyVision throughout** — unified video and documentation system
