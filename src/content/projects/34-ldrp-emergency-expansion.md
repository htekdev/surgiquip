## Project Overview

Following the success of a ceiling-mount birthing light conversion at a sister facility, this regional medical center adopted the same solution across a larger program.

## Scope of Work

### LDRP Suites (34 Rooms)

- **Skytron Ceiling Birthing Lights** — wand-controlled ceiling-mount design
- **Wand Control Systems** — wireless positioning from bedside

### Emergency Department (13 Rooms)

- **Ceiling Exam Lights** — ceiling-mount examination lights

## Project Highlights

- **Proven solution replicated** — same design that eliminated dust/jamming at a sister facility
- **Scale** — 34 LDRP suites, one of the larger birthing light installations in the Houston region
