## Project Overview

A dedicated orthopedic and spine hospital requiring equipment optimized for high-volume musculoskeletal procedures — including in-light camera integration for surgical documentation.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (10 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights with In-Light Camera** — integrated camera for surgical documentation and teaching
- **Skytron 6700B Surgical Tables** — positioning tables for orthopedic and spine procedures
- **Nurse Documentation Centers** — charting stations adjacent to surgical bays
- **SkyVision Integration** — OR video management and documentation

## Project Highlights

- **In-light camera integration** — camera integrated directly into the light head, eliminating separate camera booms
- **Orthopedic optimization** — equipment matched to the demands of orthopedic and spine surgery
- **Point-of-care documentation** — nurse documentation centers at each OR
