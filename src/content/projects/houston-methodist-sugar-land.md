## Project Overview

Houston Methodist Sugar Land represents one of Surgiquip Solutions' most comprehensive single-hospital engagements — a full-program outfitting spanning every surgical, interventional, and emergency environment in a major community hospital campus.

As the authorized Skytron dealer for Southeast Texas, Surgiquip served as the primary OR equipment contractor for the facility's build-out, coordinating procurement, phased delivery, installation, commissioning, and clinical handoff across multiple service lines simultaneously.

## Scope of Work

### Standard Operating Rooms (18 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount gas, electrical, and data service columns for anesthesia and surgical teams
- **Skytron 6700B Surgical Tables** — high-flexibility tables supporting a wide range of elective and semi-emergent surgical procedures
- **Aurora LED Surgical Lights** — consistent high-output lighting across all standard ORs
- **SkyVision Integration** — OR video management and documentation system integration

### Cardiovascular Operating Rooms (4 CV ORs)

- Heavy-duty ceiling-mount boom infrastructure supporting open-heart and vascular surgery equipment
- Aurora LED lights configured for cardiovascular visualization requirements
- Extended reach equipment booms accommodating perfusion equipment positioning

### Hybrid Operating Rooms (2 Hybrid ORs — Skytron/Siemens Zeego Integration)

- Full Skytron ceiling-mount boom system engineered around Siemens Zeego robotic C-arm footprint
- Aurora LED lights with imaging-cone clearance positioning
- Extended monitor boom arms for dual-screen angiographic imaging
- Coordination with Siemens installation team throughout construction schedule

### Catheterization Laboratory (1 Cath Lab)

- Ceiling-mount equipment carrier and service infrastructure for interventional cardiology
- Equipment layout coordinated with cardiac imaging team

### Emergency Department (4 Trauma + 12 ER Bays)

- Trauma room ceiling exam lights and equipment carriers (4 rooms)
- Emergency bay ceiling lighting — 12 standard ER treatment bays

## Project Highlights

- **Hybrid integration expertise** — Siemens Zeego robotic imaging systems require precise ceiling-mount coordination; Surgiquip managed the full integration
- **Multi-service-line coordination** — simultaneous outfitting of surgical, cardiovascular, hybrid, and emergency environments
- **SkyVision throughout standard ORs** — unified video and documentation standard across 18 rooms

## Outcome

Houston Methodist Sugar Land's full surgical and emergency program was commissioned and cleared for clinical use on schedule. The hybrid OR program — including both Siemens Zeego suites — represents one of Surgiquip's flagship interventional cardiology integration projects.
