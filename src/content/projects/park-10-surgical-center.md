## Project Overview

Park 10 Surgical Center serves the Energy Corridor and West Houston community with outpatient surgical services. Surgiquip Solutions delivered a complete surgical suite program — OR infrastructure, lights, tables, sterile processing, and nursing workflow support — under a single contract.

## Scope of Work

### Operating Rooms (4 ORs)

- **Skytron Center Mount Equipment Booms** — ceiling-mount service columns for anesthesia and surgical team gas, electrical, and data access
- **Skytron Stellar Surgical Lights** — LED illumination across all 4 ORs
- **Skytron 6700B Surgical Tables** — high-flexibility tables supporting the elective procedure mix typical of an ambulatory surgery center
- **Nurse Documentation Centers** — purpose-built nursing documentation positions reducing charting interruption during procedure

### Procedure Rooms (2 Rooms)

- Procedure-grade lighting and equipment carrier for endoscopy and minor procedure workflows

### Sterile Processing

- **Sterilizers** — autoclave sterilization for the sterile processing department

## Project Highlights

- **Nurse documentation integration** — dedicated documentation centers reduce the workflow friction of tablet or wall-mount charting during active cases
- **Single-vendor scope** — OR equipment, lights, tables, sterile processing, and nursing infrastructure under one Surgiquip contract

## Outcome

Park 10 Surgical Center opened with all surgical systems commissioned and staff trained. The full Skytron infrastructure supports a high-volume outpatient surgical program serving the West Houston market.
