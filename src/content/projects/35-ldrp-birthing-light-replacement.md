## Project Overview

Replaced a competitor's birthing light system that had been causing persistent dust accumulation and mechanical jamming during patient care.

## Scope of Work

### LDRP Suites (35 Rooms)

- **Skytron AR24 Ceiling Birthing Lights** — replacing the competitor's problematic system
- **Wand Control Systems** — wireless wand control for positioning
- **Ceiling-Mount Hardware** — structural mounting for each suite

## The Problem

The existing competitor system suffered from:
- **Dust accumulation** degrading performance over time
- **Mechanical jamming** during deliveries
- **Frequent maintenance** calls

## The Solution

The Skytron AR24 ceiling-mount design eliminates the mechanical vulnerability. Wand control provides smooth, dust-free positioning.
